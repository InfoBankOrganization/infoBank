InfoBank is an academic bank that can store and retrieve academic information for schools and students. The academic staff is allowed to input and retrieve data. The students are allowed to access their college academic website that will be connected to the infoBank. All academic topics is subject for review by the team staff for approval. If user request to view data outside their school zone. There will be a one-time fee of $19.99 for unlimited yearly access.
